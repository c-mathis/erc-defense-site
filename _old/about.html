About Us

Protecting businesses from IRS enforcement since day one.

When the ERC program launched, we saw an opportunity to help businesses recover. When the IRS started enforcement, we knew businesses would need expert defense. That's why we're here.

Our Mission

The Employee Retention Credit was designed to help businesses survive the pandemic. But the rush to claim these credits led to problems. Many businesses worked with inexperienced filers who are now gone. The IRS is ramping up enforcement. And business owners are left holding the bag.

We exist to level the playing field.

Our team combines former IRS experience with deep expertise in tax representation. We understand how the IRS thinks, how they operate, and how to protect your interests. When you work with us, you get a permanent team that will be here for the entire 5-year audit window — and beyond.
