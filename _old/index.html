FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What if my ERC filer is no longer in business?↓

Many businesses are in this position. We can step in as your successor representative, reconstruct your file, and provide the defense your original filer cannot.

Why do I need professional representation?↓

The IRS examines ERC claims with intense scrutiny. Professional representation ensures your responses are properly framed, your documentation is complete, and your rights are protected throughout the process.

Do I have to pay back the credit if audited?↓

Only if the IRS successfully challenges your eligibility. Our goal is to defend your eligibility so you keep every penny you're legally entitled to.

How long does the IRS have to audit my claim?↓

The IRS generally has 5 years from the date of filing. For most 2020/2021 claims, audits can occur through 2025-2027.

What does the free case review include?↓

We analyze your original ERC filing, identify vulnerabilities, assess your risk level, and provide recommendations — all confidentially with no obligation.
