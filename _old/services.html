Appeal Process

Administrative Appeals

If the IRS examiner proposes changes to your ERC claim, you have the right to appeal. Our team prepares comprehensive protests and represents you in appeals conferences.

IRS Appeals is an independent function that often produces favorable outcomes. We know how to present your case effectively to appeals officers who have settlement authority.

What's Included

  • ✓ Protest preparation — Written protest with legal arguments
  • ✓ Supporting documentation — Evidence compilation and presentation
  • ✓ Appeals conference — We represent you in all proceedings
  • ✓ Settlement negotiation — We pursue the best possible outcome
